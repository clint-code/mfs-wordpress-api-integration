Our Brands - Launch of InsureMe

Re-Engineering of our Technology

On May 3rd, 2021, Mobile Financial Solutions Limited changes its name to MFS Technologies Limited, in line with the company goal of being a fully integrated financial services and technology provider.

The name ‘MFS Tech’ is trade marked on June 10th, 2021

Utility Payments and E-KYC are added, bringing the total number of business components to 11

2021 marks a milestone for MFS. Using its own technology, MFS launches a first-in-class insurance solution branded InsureMe which has digitized the entire motor insurance value chain, from policy selection and purchase, vehicle and customer e-KYC, vehicle valuation and claims process.
