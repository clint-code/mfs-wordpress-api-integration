Our Origins - How And Where We Started

To be the partner of choice that enable consumers and businesses across Africa access financial services through technology.

The birth of Mobiloan

The idea of developing a digital lending solution dubbed 'Mobiloan' is conceptualized by one of the founding directors. With a skeletal staff of 2, Mobiloan is developed into a lending solution for commercial use by financial institutions.

Mobiloan trademark is registered on January 20th, 2012 and Mobile Financial Solutions (MFS) is registered on February 16th, 2012 respectively.

Our Growth - Our partners, Our Products (Mobile Lending, Mobile Banking)

MFS Anchor clients

MFS signs Kenya Commercial Bank as its first anchor client for the provision of Mobile lending services in 2013.

Stima Sacco follows suite in 2015 as the first Sacco client with the full mobile banking and digital lending solution.

The largest Telco Safaricom comes on board in 2016 to offer digital lending services to its merchants using Mobiloan.

With a growing staff of 20, MFS acquires a crucial license from the then Communications Authority of Kenya (CA) in 2017 to include PRSP services in its product offering, which also attracts more Saccos and partners.

Our Pivot - Birth of MyMobi

Re-Engineering of our Technology

MFS makes the strategic decision to shift from purely focusing on lending and mobile banking to providing the full suite of digital financial services to a wider market audience. MFS overhauls its technology architecture from a single tiered loan and mobile banking application to a micro services architecture for ease of integration, scalability and reusability. This results in a full stack end to end mobile and web application with 9 business components: Customer and Product Management, Account Services/Mobile Banking, Loans Management, Savings & Goals, Payments, Communications, Agency Banking, Insurance and Reporting & BI.

MFS makes another strategic decision to develop and build capacity around data analysis and credit scoring. MFS Data Labs Limited is registered on September 19th, 2018, and in early 2019, employs its first Data Scientist.

On March 6th, 2019, MFS trademarks the name ‘MyMobi’. Through this brand, MFS strategically diversifies its business activities and begins to provide stock and working capital financing directly to retail businesses, including beer and keg outlets, wholesalers, kiosks, salons and taxi drivers, amongst others. MFS also successfully experiments with a potato farming project in Narok County, with 30 local farmers on 60 acres of land. Produce is in excess of 700T, which is absorbed by Tropical Heat, a large FMCG manufacturer.

On April 09th, 2019, MFS trademarks the name “InsureMe’

During this period, MFS signs on Total Energies, a top Oil Marketing Company (OMC), and partners with DTB Bank, a top tier financial institution, to support its foray into retail lending activities.
