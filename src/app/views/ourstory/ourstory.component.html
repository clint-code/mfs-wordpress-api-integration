Our Origins - How And Where We Started

To be the partner of choice that enable consumers and businesses across Africa access financial services through technology.

The birth of Mobiloan

The idea of developing a digital lending solution dubbed 'Mobiloan' is conceptualized by one of the founding directors. With a skeletal staff of 2, Mobiloan is developed into a lending solution for commercial use by financial institutions.

Mobiloan trademark is registered on January 20th, 2012 and Mobile Financial Solutions (MFS) is registered on February 16th, 2012 respectively.

Our Growth - Our partners, Our Products (Mobile Lending, Mobile Banking)

To be the partner of choice that enable consumers and businesses across Africa access financial services through technology.

MFS Anchor clients

MFS signs Kenya Commercial Bank as its first anchor client for the provision of Mobile lending services in 2013.

Stima Sacco follows suite in 2015 as the first Sacco client with the full mobile banking and digital lending solution.

The largest Telco Safaricom comes on board in 2016 to offer digital lending services to its merchants using Mobiloan.

With a growing staff of 20, MFS acquires a crucial license from the then Communications Authority of Kenya (CA) in 2017 to include PRSP services in its product offering, which also attracts more Saccos and partners.

Our Pivot - Birth of MyMobi

MFS makes the strategic decision to shift from only servicing financial institutions to providing the full suite of digital financial services to a wider market audience. MFS provides stock financing directly to retail businesses involved in FMCG trade including beer and keg outlets, and a potato farming project in Narok county with the local farmers.

MFS overhauls its engineering architecture from a single tiered application to a micro services architecture for ease of integration and scalability. This gives birth to the MyMobi brand in 2020 which is a full stack end to end mobile and web application that has digitized all the banking activities with 8 Micro Services: Loan management, Payment Gateway, Data & Analytics, Communication services, Reporting & Dashboard, Digital Insurance, Agency banking and Account opening services.

MFS acquires two more partners a top Oil Marketing Company (OMC) and another top tier financial institution to support the retail lending activities.

Our Brands - Launch of InsureMe

Mobile Financial Solutions Limited changes its name to MFS Technologies Limited

2021 marks the launch of a revolutionary insurance product dubbed InsureMe; a digital insurance platform that has digitized the entire value chain of insurance from KYC to policy purchase to claims process, thus making it easier for the end customer.

MFS also expands its product suite to cover 10+ services.
